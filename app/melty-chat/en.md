# Melty Chat

Chat system in 24h.

![tofulab](/app/melty-chat/melty_chat.jpg)

## Description

Melty Chat is a messaging tool that clears messages in 24 hours.

1. Someone posts a message.
2. It vanishes 24 hours after being posted.
